Our Associated Company – Deluxe Branding

Dealex Transport & Logistics Services

Strengthening Cold Chain & Logistics Excellence

Weldedron proudly partners with Dealex Transport & Logistics Services (DTLS), a leading road transportation and logistics company with nationwide operations across Pakistan. With a modern fleet of containerized and refrigerated vehicles, Dealex specializes in the safe and efficient movement of temperature-sensitive cargo, bulk freight, and general goods.

As the operator of the Dealex Own Terminal (DOT) – Reefer & Cold Chain Hub, the company provides seamless cold chain solutions that connect businesses to local and global markets. Its advanced fleet management systems, experienced workforce, and commitment to punctuality ensure every shipment is handled with precision and care.

Integrated Competencies

  • Nationwide Logistics Backbone Across Pakistan
  • Modern Dedicated Containerized & Refrigerated Fleet
  • State-of-the-art DOT Reefer & Cold Chain Hub Operations
  • Precision Workforce & Live Fleet Tracking Modules

Together, Weldedron and Dealex deliver integrated logistics solutions combining infrastructure, fleet reliability, and operational excellence.
